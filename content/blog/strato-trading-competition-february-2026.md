We're back with another exciting competition for our STRATO community! This time, we're putting your trading skills to the test. Whether you're a savvy trader or a community builder, there are two ways to win real silver.

## Competition Overview

This trading competition is designed for new participants looking to showcase their trading abilities on STRATO. With two distinct winning categories, everyone has a fair shot at taking home a prize.

### Two Ways to Win

| Category | Criteria | Prize |
|----------|----------|-------|
| **Best Trader** | Highest % gain from starting balance | 1 oz Silver |
| **Top Referrer** | Most qualifying referrals | 1 oz Silver |

**Total Prize Pool: 2 oz of Silver**

## Entry & Eligibility

Getting started is straightforward:

1. **Create a new STRATO account** to participate
2. **Fund your account** with a minimum of $10 via bridge-in or transfer
3. **<a href="https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSc_VlxIXe5vczAmIwIoHtAsSj3xAqiQfsnx7wrHZ-DZdLyZMQ/viewform" target="_blank">Submit the entry form</a>** to lock in your starting balance
4. **Start trading** once the competition begins

**Important:** Your starting balance is locked the moment you submit the entry form. This ensures a fair playing field for all participants.

## Competition Period

**February 2 – February 17, 2026 (ends at 12pm EST)**

During the competition period, the following restrictions apply:

- No bridge-ins allowed
- No transfers allowed
- Trading only with your locked starting balance

This ensures that all gains are purely from trading activity, not additional capital.

## Winner Categories Explained

### Highest % Gain

This category rewards skilled traders who can grow their portfolio through smart trading decisions.

- Winner is determined by **percentage increase** from starting balance
- A $10 account that grows to $15 (50% gain) beats a $100 account that grows to $140 (40% gain)
- Only trading gains count — no additional deposits allowed

### Most Referrals

This category rewards community builders who bring new users to STRATO.

- Each qualifying referral must **create a new account**
- Each referral must **bridge in a minimum of $10**
- The participant with the most qualifying referrals wins

## Disqualification Rules

To maintain fair competition, the following actions will result in disqualification:

- **Adding funds** during the competition period (bridge-ins or transfers)
- **Operating multiple accounts**
- **Referral abuse** or fraudulent referral activity

We actively monitor for these violations to ensure a level playing field.
