## Overview

As Head of Ecosystem at BlockApps, Bob Summerwill is helping drive STRATO’s transition from a closed-source platform to a rich, decentralized ecosystem—bridging on-chain and real-world assets.

## Community and history work

Bob also leads the [Early Days of Ethereum](https://earlydaysofeth.org/) history project, including extensive content and an ongoing [conversations series](https://earlydaysofeth.org/conversations).

He regularly speaks at conferences; see his schedule at [bobsummerwill.com/schedule](https://bobsummerwill.com/schedule/).

## Background

Bob has been programming since 1984, starting at age ten. Originally from the UK, he has lived in Vancouver since 2003 and is now a dual citizen of Canada and the UK.
