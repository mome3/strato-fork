## Overview

Victor Wong is Co-Founder and Chief Product Officer at BlockApps and STRATO. He is a blockchain veteran with over a decade of experience driving mainstream adoption.

## Web3 and enterprise adoption

An early contributor to Ethereum and [ConsenSys](https://consensys.io/), Victor helped co-create Blockchain-as-a-Service, supported the establishment of the [Enterprise Ethereum Alliance](https://entethalliance.com/), and developed some of the first real-world assets on private Ethereum blockchains.

These assets supported Fortune 500 companies and governments across sectors including mining, real estate, and agriculture.

## Earlier ventures

Before Web3, Victor was the tech founder of:
- [SmarTots](https://web.archive.org/web/20120503030930/http://www.smartots.com/), a mobile family startup
- [Speak2Me](https://web.archive.org/web/20130327042400/http://www.speak2me.cn/), a language-learning social network
