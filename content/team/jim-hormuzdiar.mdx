## Overview

Jim Hormuzdiar is the Co-Founder and Chief Technology Officer of [BlockApps](https://blockapps.net/) and [STRATO](https://strato.nexus/), bringing deep technical expertise in blockchain architecture and distributed systems.

## Building STRATO since 2014

As a co-founder of BlockApps since 2014, Jim has helped build the technical foundation of the STRATO blockchain platform—one of the earliest enterprise blockchain solutions.

## What he focuses on

At STRATO, Jim leads the technical team in developing secure, scalable infrastructure for digital asset finance, ensuring the platform can handle complex financial operations while maintaining high standards of security and reliability.

## Vision

Jim’s approach combines cutting-edge blockchain technology with practical business applications, creating solutions that connect traditional finance with decentralized systems.
