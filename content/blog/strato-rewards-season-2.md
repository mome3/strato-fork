Season 2 of the STRATO Rewards Program is here — and it's bigger than ever.

Building on the foundation of our [inaugural rewards campaign](/blog/introducing-strato-rewards), Season 2 introduces **10 emission-earning activities** spanning liquidity provision, CDP minting, savings, bridging, and vault deposits. Whether you've been earning since day one or you're just getting started, there's never been a better time to put your assets to work on STRATO.

## What's New in Season 2

Season 2 expands the rewards program with a broader range of earning opportunities and a new points emission system. Activities are now organized into clear categories, each designed to incentivize the actions that matter most to protocol health and growth.

The headline addition is **saveUSDST** — a new ERC-4626 savings vault that lets you deposit USDST and earn yield automatically. It's the simplest way to earn on STRATO, and it's fully integrated into the rewards program.

## Season 2 Activities

### Liquidity Provision (6 Activities)

Providing liquidity to swap pools remains one of the most impactful ways to strengthen the STRATO ecosystem — and Season 2 rewards it accordingly. Six swap LP pools are now eligible for points:

- **ETHST-USDST Swap LP**
- **GOLDST-USDST Swap LP**
- **SILVST-USDST Swap LP**
- **sUSDS-USDST Swap LP**
- **syrupUSDC-USDST Swap LP**
- **USDC-USDT-USDST Swap LP**

Each pool earns points continuously based on your share of total staked value. The more liquidity you provide, the more you earn.

### CDP, Savings & Vault (3 Activities)

- **CDP USDST Mint** — Mint USDST using collateral and earn points for the duration of your position.
- **saveUSDST Stake** — Deposit USDST into the new savings vault and earn yield plus rewards simultaneously.
- **Vault Token Deposits** — Deposit vault tokens to earn ongoing rewards.

### Bridging (1 Activity)

- **Direct Mint USDST** — Bridge funds into STRATO by minting USDST directly and earn points on your position.

## Points Emission at a Glance

Every activity has a defined **emission rate** — the number of points distributed daily across all participants. Your share depends on your stake relative to the pool. Here's a snapshot of current rates:

Activities like Direct Mint USDST and the USDC-USDT-USDST Swap LP each emit **1,111.11 points per day**, while saveUSDST Stake and GOLDST-USDST Swap LP each emit **555.56 points per day**. Estimated Incentive APY varies by pool and total stake — early participants benefit from higher rates.

## How to Start Earning

1. **Navigate to the Rewards page** from the PRO category on STRATO.
2. **Choose an activity** — review estimated APY, emission rates, and total stake for each option.
3. **Enter a position** — deposit, mint, stake, or provide liquidity.
4. **Track your points** in real time from the Rewards dashboard.
5. **Claim your rewards** whenever you're ready — no minimums, no lockups.
