Our momentum continues at STRATO! After an exciting first referral contest, we're thrilled to launch our next community competition — this time rewarding users for bridging stables from Ethereum to STRATO.

If you've been looking for the perfect time to move assets on-chain and put them to work, this is it.

## Save on STRATO & Win

Now it's time for our second contest, focused on growing liquidity and activity on STRATO through Ethereum bridging.

### Contest Goal

Deposit stables from Ethereum to STRATO through Easy Save during the contest period to earn entries into a prize drawing.

**The more you bridge — and keep bridged — the more chances you have to win.**

## How It Works

Participating is simple:

1. **Log into the STRATO app** and navigate to your dashboard
2. **Navigate to the Deposit section**
3. **Deposit USDT or USDC** from Ethereum to STRATO and make sure "Earn saving rate by offering USDST for lending" is checked
4. **Earn entries automatically** based on your earned pts during the campaign period

## Entry Rules

Every **one pt earned = 1 entry**

For details on how pts are accumulated and calculated, see our [STRATO Rewards Program guide](/blog/introducing-strato-rewards/).

Entries are calculated as:

> **Point accrued** during the contest period

**Important details:**

- Only assets remaining bridged on STRATO at the end of the contest are counted
- There is no limit to the number of entries you can earn

## Contest Period

**January 23 – January 30, 2026**

The contest ends at **11:59 AM ET on January 30th**.

All bridge activity must occur within this window to count.

## Prize Pool

One lucky winner will receive:

- **10% of the total net bridged amount** during the contest period
- **Maximum prize cap: $10,000**

Prize amount is calculated based on total net bridged value across all participants. The more the community bridges, the larger the prize — up to the cap.

## Why Bridge to STRATO?

By bridging assets to STRATO, you unlock access to:

- **Real-world asset (RWA) tokens** backed by gold (GOLDST) and silver (SILVST)
- **Collateralized borrowing** against hard assets
- **Liquidity pools** and DeFi utilities
- **A seamless platform** designed specifically for asset-backed finance

And now — you're rewarded just for getting started.

## Tips to Maximize Your Chances

- **Avoid bridging assets out** during the contest period
- **Bridge earlier** to ensure transactions settle in time
- **Bridge larger amounts** to increase your total entries
- **Keep assets in lending vault** through the end of the competition
