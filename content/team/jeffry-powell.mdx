## Overview

Jeffry Powell is a seasoned enterprise-technology executive with a track record of shaping go-to-market strategies for emerging infrastructure platforms at the intersection of blockchain, tokenization, and real-world assets.

## Role at BlockApps

At BlockApps, Jeff helps advance the deployment and market positioning of STRATO, a Web3 asset infrastructure enabling tokenization, staking, trading, and redemption of physical and real-world assets such as gold, silver, carbon offsets, and collectibles.

His commercial leadership focuses on:
- Defining clear value propositions for tokenized, vault-backed assets
- Building strategic alliances and partnerships
- Driving adoption with enterprise-grade scalability, identity verification, and asset controls
- Expanding access to markets historically limited to large institutions

## Prior experience

Before BlockApps, Jeff held senior leadership roles at [Diligent Corporation](https://www.diligent.com/), a global provider of governance, risk, and compliance software. He worked closely with corporate boards, executives, and compliance teams to align regulatory, technology, and operational requirements—helping organizations improve governance, manage risk transparently, and adopt digital workflows.

## What motivates him

Jeff blends strategic vision with hands-on execution and is passionate about digital ownership, market infrastructure, and how blockchain can unlock new asset classes, improve transparency, and reshape how value is exchanged globally—aligned with STRATO’s promise of “Where Stability Meets Opportunity.”
